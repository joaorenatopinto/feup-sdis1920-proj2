Ferrari are in advanced negotiations with Carlos Sainz for the Spaniard to replace Sebastian Vettel at the team for 2021.

Sky Sports understands that talks are at a detailed stage for the under-contract McLaren driver.

Daniel Ricciardo, out of contract at Renault at the end of this year, is McLaren's planned replacement for Sainz.
